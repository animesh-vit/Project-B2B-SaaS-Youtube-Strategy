## Post 1

**Date:** June 23, 2026

**Main topic:**  
Whether B2B buyers are on YouTube.

**Key insights:**

- Most B2B audiences are on YouTube, but volume depends on TAM size.
- B2B channels should not compare themselves to broad B2C creators.
- A few hundred views from the right ICP can be more valuable than mass-market reach.
- Very narrow ICPs may not get consistent organic leads, but YouTube can still support sales.

**Actionable takeaway for B2B SaaS YouTube strategy:**

- Size the audience before setting goals.
- If search demand is strong, use YouTube as a lead source.
- If the ICP is tiny, use videos as sales enablement before calls, in follow-ups, and on the website.

## Post 2

**Date:** 22 June, 2026

**Main topic:**  
Turning an underused SaaS YouTube channel into a lead gen channel.

**Key insights:**

- Many SaaS companies already have strong content but treat YouTube like storage.
- Podcasts, webinars, newsletters, and LinkedIn posts can become focused YouTube assets.
- YouTube needs intentional titles, thumbnails, hooks, structure, and CTAs.
- Distribution through LinkedIn and email can create ROI before organic YouTube compounds.

**Actionable takeaway for B2B SaaS YouTube strategy:**

- Repurpose existing content into YouTube-native videos instead of uploading raw recordings.
- Build content buckets from proven topics, buyer pain, and existing high-performing assets.
- Use a two-CTA system: one for deeper trust, one for booked calls.

## Post 3

**Date:** 19 June, 2026

**Main topic:**  
Sustainable YouTube execution for lead generation.

**Key insights:**

- Editing, SEO, and packaging matter, but they are baseline requirements.
- The bigger challenge is whether YouTube fits the company's operating rhythm.
- Most companies quit too early, before YouTube has time to compound.
- Consistency over 12 months matters more than one strong video.

**Actionable takeaway for B2B SaaS YouTube strategy:**

- Design a YouTube workflow the founder or team can sustain for a year.
- Solve production consistency before obsessing over polish.
- Use partners, systems, or repurposing to reduce burnout and keep publishing.
